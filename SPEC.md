# Product Plan: Grow Therapy Mental Health Platform

## Project Goal
Create a platform that connects therapists with clients, focusing on insurance integration and provider management.

## Market Analysis
- **Target Audience**: Licensed therapists and individuals seeking mental health services.
- **Core Value**: Reducing the administrative burden on therapists while making care accessible to clients.

## Functional Specifications
- **Provider Matching**: Algorithmic matching based on specialty, insurance, and availability.
- **Insurance Verification**: Automated checking of client insurance coverage.
- **Scheduling System**: Integrated calendar for booking and rescheduling.
- **Billing Automation**: Seamless processing of insurance claims and co-pays.

## Success Metrics
- **Match Rate**: Percentage of clients finding a suitable therapist within 48 hours.
- **Provider Retention**: Number of therapists using the platform for their primary practice.
